Hi, I’m Maho and when developing user experience,


user-centered mindset is second nature for me.

I can get passionate about very subtle details.

I care about user’s non-verbal response.

I like to make sure information is clearly presented.

Coming from a music therapy background...

One thing I find in common between designing therapy sessions as a music therapist and designing user experience as a UX/UI designer, is the importance of the designer’s ability to empathize with the clients (or the users) in delivering a successful and meaningful experience to meet their needs.

UX Skills

User interviews, User Testing, Affinity Diagram, Empathy Map and User Persona development, Heuristic Evaluation, Journey Map and Story Board creation, Site Map, Task and User Flow development, Card Sorting, Wireframing and Prototyping.

Human (Soft) Skills

Building rapport, behavioral observation and analysis, general friendliness, critical thinking, giving constructive feedback/supervision, team oriented, confidence with adaptability and resilience. Mindful of cultural differences and accessibility for those with special needs.

Design Tools

Adobe XD, InVision, Miro Board, Figma, Adobe Draw, Photoshop, Illustrator, Final Cut Pro
