Resume

Summary

A frontend developer, with a background in music therapy, trained at Rice University’s UX/UI bootcamp. Proficient with HTML5, CSS, Javascript, Bootstrap, Angular, Vue, and Node.js. As a frontend developer in a small startup, successfully designed UI and developed both static and dynamic websites catered for scientists at various research laboratories. Strengths lie in the coexisting empathetic user oriented communication skills garnered through years of working and teaching as a music therapist, and problem solving skills as a developer to translate the design idea into real products clients are using daily. I take pride in shipping out user interfaces that clients can rely on for their daily routine.
