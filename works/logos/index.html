Logo Designs

The name of this business in Japanese means “to open”, and is a pun referring to a protective Egyptian deity, Aker, and the office’s location - located on a street with a name meaning “not opening”. The graphic design focuses on the Egyptian god’s meaning of the business name. The muted color was chosen to fit the city of Kyoto’s business landscaping guideline in case the office wants to put up a sign in the front.
